Childhood Memories

The Kingdom Hearts Series

Back when I was a teenager in private school, I was introduced to the game that would change my interest and views on videogames. This game is called Kingdom Hearts and was played on the PlayStation 2.

The .hack// Series

The .hack// series took what my interest in story driven videogames and fully took off like a hyped up fighter jet. Funny thing, I actually played the second set before I played the first. I started my journey into this series with the .hack//G.U. series and aside from the first game in the series the second game was the very first game I purchased with my own money.
